I studied the first 2 years of engineering in software design in my hometown, after moving I enrolled in a career as similar as possible to the one I was studying before.

When I was about to start my first year of university in the new city of Puerto Madryn, the coronavirus shook the world and plunged us into strict isolation in our homes. But the world cannot stop, so universities, jobs and hobbies are now carried out on the internet through devices such as smart phones or computers.

At this point in my life is where I fell in love with the web since it is the perfect medium by which I can develop code for different devices, without having to worry about their hardware or operating system differences. So I got down to work and started a series of virtual courses certified by the National Technological University in Argentina.

Everything you can see on this website is thanks to my knowledge acquired throughout my life in a self-taught way and largely from the universities where I studied different technologies until I found what I like, which is web programming.
